COLUMN 16: description_raw  ← MOST CRITICAL
  Length:  100 to 180 words per job
  
  THIS IS WHAT SBERT ENCODES FOR THE JOB SIDE.
  Matching quality depends entirely on this text.
  
  3 writing styles (mix across 500 jobs):
  
  STYLE 1 — Professional JD (200 jobs):
    "TechCorp is hiring a Data Analyst intern to join our
     FinTech team in Mumbai. You will work on building
     financial data pipelines, customer behavior analysis,
     and automated reporting dashboards. Strong proficiency
     in Python and SQL is required, along with hands-on
     experience with Pandas for data manipulation.
     Knowledge of Power BI or Tableau for visualization
     is a plus. We are looking for candidates with a
     minimum GPA of 6.5 and genuine interest in financial
     technology. Duration is 3 months with a stipend of
     ₹15000 per month. Freshers are welcome to apply."
  
  STYLE 2 — Startup tone (150 jobs):
    "Hey! We're StartupX — a fast-growing EdTech startup
     looking for a passionate Frontend Developer intern.
     You'll be building React components, improving our
     learning dashboard UI, and shipping features fast.
     You should know React, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS well.
     TypeScript and Tailwind experience is a bonus.
     We offer ₹12000/month, full remote work, and you'll
     actually own real product features — not just bug fixes.
     If you have side projects or open-source contributions,
     we want to see them. GPA not our primary filter —
     your GitHub is. 2-month internship. Apply now."
  
  STYLE 3 — Technical detailed (150 jobs):
    "We are seeking a Machine Learning Engineer intern to
     support our computer vision research team at VisionAI.
     Responsibilities include training and evaluating deep
     learning models using TensorFlow and PyTorch, preprocessing
     image datasets, and implementing model optimization techniques.
     The candidate must have solid understanding of convolutional
     neural networks and experience with Python and NumPy.
     Prior work on Kaggle competitions or research projects
     in Computer Vision is highly valued. Minimum CGPA 7.0.
     Candidates with published papers or open-source ML
     contributions will be prioritized. Onsite, Bangalore.
     6-month internship. Stipend ₹25000 per month."
  
  CRITICAL RULES (same as resume_text_raw):
    1. Every job description must be unique
    2. Use synonyms naturally:
       "data wrangling" = "Pandas data manipulation"
       "containerization" = "Docker"
       "cloud deployment" = "AWS/GCP"
    3. Mention domain context (FinTech, HealthTech etc)
       This helps SBERT understand domain matching
    4. Include numbers: stipend, duration, GPA
    5. Mention work culture: remote, startup, research-focused
